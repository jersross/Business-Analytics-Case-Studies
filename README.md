# Business-Analytics-Case-Studies

## Case Study 1 – Pizza Production Optimization (Arrowmark Vending)
- Collaborated in a team to analyze optimal production levels under uncertain demand for game-day pizza sales  
- Applied Expected Monetary Value (EMV) to evaluate profitability across multiple demand and production scenarios  
- Incorporated cost structures (fixed, variable) and probabilistic demand distributions into decision modeling  
- Identified profit-maximizing strategy: producing 500 cheese and 500 pepperoni pizzas per game  
- Balanced operational risks of overproduction (waste) vs. underproduction (lost sales)  
- Delivered data-driven recommendations and an actionable plan for adjusting production based on real-world factors (e.g., attendance, weather)  
- Strengthened skills in decision analysis, modeling under uncertainty, and team-based problem solving  

## Case Study 2 – Stock Option Valuation & Financial Modeling (MHP)
- Worked collaboratively to evaluate the fair value of employee stock options using financial modeling techniques  
- Compared two valuation approaches: expected value method vs. Binomial Asset Pricing Model (risk-neutral valuation)  
- Applied core finance concepts including time value of money, present value, and no-arbitrage principles  
- Identified significant overvaluation in subjective probability models and demonstrated arbitrage opportunities  
- Analyzed investment risk, payoff structures, and strategic financial decision-making  
- Provided recommendations on compensation evaluation and risk exposure for stock-based incentives  
- Strengthened understanding of financial markets, derivatives pricing, and analytical teamwork
